Research@YDSP 2025

Hey there, future innovator! Are you ready to dive into real-world engineering and tech projects? Research@YDSP is your chance to step into a 4-month journey (Sep to Dec) where you’ll work alongside actual engineers and explore exciting fields in Engineering and Infocomm Technology.

Here’s what’s in store for you:

  • Pick up to two projects you’re most excited about and rank them by preference.
  • Write a short piece (100–200 words) on why these projects inspire you—this will help mentors match you to the right challenge.
  • Some projects may even have a virtual interview to get to know you better.
  • Make sure to send in your application by 21 Jul 2025 (Mon).

Your attachment starts part-time during school term and shifts to full-time for 2–4 weeks in the holidays (don’t worry—it’s flexible and won’t clash with your school work). By the end, you’ll showcase your work in a report, a poster, and maybe even present at YDSP Congress 2026 in April!

What’s the timeline?

  • Now – 3rd week of Jul: Choose and apply for projects
  • 4th week of Jul – Mid Aug: Shortlisting and mentor confirmation
  • Sep – Dec: Start your attachment and make an impact
